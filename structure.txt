Pipeline 2-Stage (Thứ tự chạy):

1. Làm sạch dữ liệu: src/data_prep/data_cleaning.py
2. Chuyển COCO → YOLO:
   a. Binary (Stage 1): src/Training_dataYolo.py (BINARY_MODE=True)
   b. 5 classes (Stage 2): src/Training_dataYolo.py (BINARY_MODE=False)
3. Chia train/val/test: src/data_prep/split_dataset.py
4. Tiling cho Stage 1: src/data_prep/tiling.py
5. Crop BBox cho Stage 2: src/data_prep/crop_for_classification.py
6. (Optional) Gộp dataset ngoài: src/data_prep/merge_external_datasets.py
7. Train Stage 1 (YOLO): src/train_stage1_detector.py
8. Train Stage 2 (EfficientNet): src/train_stage2_classifier.py
9. Inference kết hợp: src/inference_2stage.py
10. Đánh giá: src/evaluate_2stage.py
